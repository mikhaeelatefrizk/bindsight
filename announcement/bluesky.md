## Cross-posting strategy

You can post to all three platforms (X / LinkedIn / BlueSky) within the same
day. Recommended order:

1. **X / Twitter first** (most viral; gets the conversation started)
2. **BlueSky 1 hour later** (technical audience; often picks up the thread
   from X reposts)
3. **LinkedIn last, ~3 hours later** (longer-form, thought-leadership tone)

If you want to write once and post everywhere, the BlueSky single-post
variant works on all three platforms with minor formatting tweaks.
